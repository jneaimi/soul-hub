# Activity Recommendations — Tokyo, Japan

**Conditions:** 6°C (feels like 2.6°C) · Clear sky · Night · Humidity 55% · Wind 6.6 km/h · Gusts up to 40.3 km/h

---

## Recommended Activities

### Indoor
- **Izakaya or ramen dinner** — Perfect weather for warming up with hot food and drinks at a cozy restaurant.
- **Museum or gallery visit** — Tokyo has excellent late-night options (Mori Art Museum, teamLab venues).
- **Onsen or sento** — A hot bath is ideal when it feels like 2.6°C outside.
- **Karaoke** — Classic Tokyo night activity, fully indoor and warm.
- **Shopping in a covered mall** — Shibuya Hikarie, Roppongi Hills, or any covered arcade (shotengai).

### Outdoor (with precautions)
- **Night photography** — Clear sky makes for great cityscape shots; dress in layers and brace for gusts.
- **Evening stroll in Shinjuku Gyoen or Ueno** — Manageable if you're dressed warmly; wind gusts of 40 km/h can be strong.
- **Convenience store food tour** — Short outdoor bursts between warm stops work well in this weather.

---

## What to Avoid
- **Long outdoor exposure without wind protection** — Gusts up to 40.3 km/h combined with 2.6°C windchill makes it feel harsh quickly.
- **Cycling or open-air rides** — Wind gusts are high enough to be uncomfortable and affect stability.

---

## Dressing Advice
Layer up: thermal base layer, mid-layer fleece, windproof outer shell. Gloves and a beanie are recommended given the windchill.
